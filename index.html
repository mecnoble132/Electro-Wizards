Our Story

We are a team of 7th-grade innovators from Alan Feldman Public School, Kerala, India. We all share a passion for tinkering with electronics.


Our goal is to learn and explore the exciting world of circuits, components, and coding. We love transforming ideas into real projects! Our website showcases the electronic projects we have built together. We hope to inspire others by sharing our work.


We want to ignite young minds and push boundaries in the world of electronics. Our projects are a testament to our ingenuity and enthusiasm.


We invite you to explore our website to see what we have built. Join us on our electrifying adventure!
